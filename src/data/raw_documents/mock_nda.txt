MUTUAL NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT

This Mutual Non-Disclosure Agreement (the "Agreement") is entered into as of April 18, 2026, by and between 
Apex Quantitative Strategies LLC ("Disclosing Party") and the Undersigned Evaluator ("Receiving Party"). 

WHEREAS, the Disclosing Party has developed proprietary quantitative trading models, specifically involving 
algorithmic execution, statistical arbitrage, and mean reversion strategies optimized for commodity futures 
and index markets. The Parties wish to explore a potential business relationship regarding the product management 
and institutional deployment of these proprietary systems (the "Purpose").

1. DEFINITION OF CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION. "Confidential Information" shall mean any and all technical and non-technical 
information provided by the Disclosing Party, including but not limited to: source code, order flow analysis parameters, 
Depth of Market (DOM) integration logic, footprint analysis thresholds, backtesting datasets, Sharpe and Sortino ratio 
evaluations, and strategic business plans. Confidential Information shall not include information that is currently in 
the public domain or becomes publicly known through no fault of the Receiving Party.

2. OBLIGATIONS OF RECEIVING PARTY. The Receiving Party agrees to hold and maintain the Confidential Information in 
strictest confidence for the sole and exclusive benefit of the Disclosing Party. The Receiving Party shall restrict 
access to Confidential Information to employees, contractors, and third parties as is reasonably required to fulfill 
the Purpose, and shall not, without prior written approval of Disclosing Party, use for Receiving Party's own benefit, 
publish, copy, or otherwise disclose to others, or permit the use by others for their benefit or to the detriment of 
Disclosing Party, any Confidential Information. 

3. TERM AND GOVERNING LAW. The nondisclosure provisions of this Agreement shall survive the termination of this Agreement 
and Receiving Party's duty to hold Confidential Information in confidence shall remain in effect until the Confidential 
Information no longer qualifies as a trade secret. This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with 
the laws of the State of Georgia, without regard to its conflict of law principles.
